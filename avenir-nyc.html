About

Built from
inside the work

Avenir NYC is a boutique strategy consultancy focused on education and workforce development in New York City — founded by Eliza, a strategist who has spent more than a decade inside the organizations this work is built to serve.

That experience includes building a nonprofit from one staff member to twenty and growing its budget by 400%, and leading data-informed strategic planning inside NYC Public Schools — translating between what programs do, what data shows, and what decision-makers need to act. The work at Avenir draws directly from that, not from frameworks applied from the outside, but from what actually moves things forward inside complex, resource-constrained environments.

Every engagement is designed to leave your team more capable — with clearer direction, stronger skills, and the tools to keep moving after the work is done.
